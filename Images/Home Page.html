Why Tedibar

Your baby’s skin needs special care, which is why Tedibar is designed with gentle formulas for sensitive skin.

Tedibar is the preferred choice of paediatrician. It gently cleanses your baby’s skin without causing irritation.

Baby skin is extra prone to dryness. Tedibar bathing bar has moisturising formula that maintains protective skin mantle.
 

Tedibar bathing bar is completely soap-free to protect against irritation. It acts as a gentle cleanser that keeps your baby’s skin soft and supple. 

Baby skin has a higher pH level than adult skin, which is why Tedibar bathing bar is perfectly pH balanced to suit your baby’s delicate skin.
